Panel Description

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity using silicon cells. Types include monocrystalline, polycrystalline, and thin-film, each varying in efficiency and cost. They lower electricity bills and have minimal maintenance. Installation requires optimal location and angle. Future trends include improved efficiency and better energy storage.
